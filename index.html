DIMA


DIMA is a NGO in Kerala, which was formed for  
providing disaster response & preparedness services across India. As a disaster relief agency, we help people to survive and rebuild their lives through food, medical, education, shelter and livelihood programs.

We do 

RAF

We provides Rapid Action Force in natural disaster areas. We reach their on time and as fast and we can., and we provide volunteer services in rescue missions

Recovery

Provides financial services for affected people and helps to rebuild their life once again.

Health Services

We also provides timely use of personal health services to achieve the best possible health outcomes
